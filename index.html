Biography of A R Rahman

A R Rahman is a famous Indian music composer, singer-songwriter, and music producer. He is one of the most successful music composers in the world. He has won several awards for his work, including two Academy Awards, two Grammy Awards, a BAFTA Award, a Golden Globe, four National Film Awards, fifteen Filmfare Awards, and sixteen Filmfare Awards South. He has also been awarded the Padma Bhushan, the third-highest civilian award in India, in 2010 by the Government of India. He is also known as the "Mozart of Madras" and "Isai Puyal" (the Musical Storm) by his fans and the media. He has been described as the world's most prominent and prolific film composer by Time. In 2009, Time placed Rahman in its list of World's Most Influential People.

Rahman's works are noted for integrating Indian classical music with electronic music, world music, and traditional orchestral arrangements.
